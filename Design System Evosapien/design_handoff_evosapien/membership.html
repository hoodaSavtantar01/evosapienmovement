Questions

Frequently Asked.

Do I need experience to join?

No. We work with complete beginners and competitive athletes alike. Every new member starts with a personal assessment so the program meets you where you are.

What does the first week look like?

Your first week includes a movement assessment, baseline strength markers, an introduction to the climbing wall, and a recovery walkthrough. Programming is written the same week and reviewed monthly.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes — up to 60 days per calendar year for medical, travel, or work reasons. No reactivation fee.

Do you offer corporate or family plans?

Yes. Corporate plans (5+ members) and family plans (2+) receive preferred rates. Contact us directly to discuss.
